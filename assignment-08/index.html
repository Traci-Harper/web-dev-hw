DIY Cloud Light

Materials Needed:

  • Cotton batting material
  • Paper lanterns
  • Wooden dowel
  • Fishing Line
  • Hot glue gun
  • LED string lights
  • Screw hooks

Step One:

Use the glue gun to apply the hot glue on the paper lantern. Then start attaching the cotton.

Step Two:

Using your wooden dowel, tie each ball individually and adjust the length as needed.

Step Three:

Finally, pull the LED lights through the base of the cloud. Enjoy your new cloud!
